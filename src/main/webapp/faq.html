FAQ

If I read the following FAQ do I need to read the full terms of use?

The purpose of the FAQ is to describe in simple terms the terms of use that must be upheld when navigating the PharmaSea website.


How do I accept the terms of use?

The use of any feature of the site automatically means that you agree to all the terms and conditions of PharmaSea.


If I agree to the terms of use, does that mean that I am a partner of PharmaSea or do I have an obligation to PharmaSea?

No, acceptance of the terms does not mean that you are an affiliate of PharmaSea. It means that you agree to abide by these terms when browsing the site.


How do I become a member of PharmaSea?

To become a member of PharmaSea you need to register by entering your email account and choosing a username of your choice.


If my account is blocked or deleted, do I have the right to register or use PharmaSea services again?

No. If your account is blocked or deleted, you are not entitled to use the services of a member of the page again, unless you are allowed to do so by PharmaSea. You may, however, use the general services of the page as a simple anonymous visitor.


Does PharmaSea transfer my personal data to third parties without my consent?

No. Your personal data is not transferred for any reason to any third party without your consent, unless required by the relevant legislation and competent authorities.
