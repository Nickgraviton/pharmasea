Cookies

Cookies are small files that are sent to and stored in the application you use to browse the internet. Each website usually uses one or more cookies where it stores information that it deems necessary for the best possible functioning of the website.
We use cookies of the following categories:

  • Required
    The lack of cookies in this category greatly affects the functioning of the website. Without cookies, functions such as logging in as a member to a website, online shopping or payments are not possible.
  • Performance
    Performance cookies are related to the collection of information about the behaviour of users while they are browsing the website and store anonymous information about usage statistics, performance, etc. Their purpose is to collect information that improves the operation of the website.
